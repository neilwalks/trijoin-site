# Trident Joinery Services Marketing Plan

## Positioning

Lead with staircase refurbishments, especially oak and glass transformations. Treat the other good earners as supporting work: under-stair storage, door hanging, loft storage and access, kitchen fitting, driveway gates, then wider joinery.

Primary line:

**Oak & Glass Staircase Refurbishment Specialists in Wirral**

Supporting line:

**Bespoke staircase transformations, glass balustrades, oak cladding, under-stair storage, door hanging, loft storage, kitchen fitting, driveway gates and joinery across Wirral, Liverpool, Cheshire and North Wales.**

## Website Priorities

1. Home page focused on oak and glass staircase transformations.
2. Dedicated page for oak and glass staircase refurbishments.
3. Dedicated page for glass balustrades.
4. Before-and-after gallery with real project photos.
5. Supporting services section covering under-stair storage, door hanging, loft storage/access, kitchen fitting, driveway gates and other joinery.
6. Loft doors included under loft storage and access rather than overpromoted as a standalone offer.
7. Simple enquiry route: call, quote form and optional WhatsApp.

## Social Plan Without Blogging

Post 2 or 3 times per week. Use real job photos and short captions.

### Weekly Pattern

Monday: before-and-after staircase transformation.

Wednesday: detail shot of oak, glass, handrail, newel post or finish.

Friday: final reveal or short progress video.

### Caption Template

Oak and glass staircase refurbishment completed in [area].

Existing staircase upgraded with oak finishes and glass balustrades for a cleaner, brighter hallway.

For quotes, call Trident Joinery Services on 07921 188 800.

### Reel Ideas

- 5-second before shot, 5-second fitting progress, 5-second final reveal.
- Close-up sweep along oak handrail and glass panels.
- Old spindles removed, glass panels fitted, final hallway reveal.
- Customer reaction or finished walkthrough.

## Photo Checklist For Every Stair Job

- Straight-on before photo from bottom of stairs.
- Before photo from top landing.
- Close-up of old spindles or handrail.
- Mid-job progress photo.
- Finished photo from bottom of stairs.
- Finished photo from top landing.
- Close-up of oak handrail.
- Close-up of glass panel and fixing detail.
