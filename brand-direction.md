# Trident Joinery Services Brand Direction

## Recommended Positioning

Trident should be positioned as a staircase refurbishment and practical joinery specialist, with oak and glass transformations as the lead service.

The look should stay:

- Charcoal
- Oak timber
- Warm white
- Pale glass blue

This keeps the brand feeling premium, practical and trade-professional without looking too glossy or fake.

## Recommended Tagline

**Modern staircases. Better storage. Proper joinery.**

Why this works:

- It leads with staircases.
- It naturally includes under-stair storage and loft storage.
- It keeps the no-nonsense trade feel.
- It avoids sounding like a generic building company.

## Other Tagline Options

- **Oak, glass and proper joinery.**
- **Staircase transformations and bespoke joinery.**
- **Transforming staircases, storage and homes.**
- **Crafted staircases. Practical joinery.**
- **Modern oak and glass staircase specialists.**

## Logo Direction

The preferred direction is now a clean oak leaf mark:

- The oak leaf signals oak, timber, strength and craftsmanship.
- The simple shape stays readable at small sizes on the website, social profiles, workwear and vehicle graphics.
- The Trident name can carry the identity without forcing a trident shape into the icon.

A full tree could work for a heritage-style brand, but it risks feeling more like landscaping, forestry or a general countryside business. For Trident, an oak leaf is sharper and more trade-friendly.

The full logo direction uses:

- A trident-inspired timber mark.
- Oak and charcoal colours.
- A strong, simple wordmark.
- A supporting line: Staircase Refurbishment Specialists.

This works better than a decorative logo because it will stay readable on vans, workwear, social profiles, quotes, invoices and the website.

## Brand Voice

Keep the wording confident and practical:

- Skilled
- Local
- Tidy
- Reliable
- Detail-focused
- No gimmicks

Avoid sounding too corporate or too influencer-style. The brand should feel like a high-quality local specialist who knows exactly what they are doing.
